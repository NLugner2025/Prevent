Prevent Strategy

The Prevent strategy is a key part of the UK's counter-terrorism strategy, aimed at preventing individuals from becoming radicalized and supporting terrorism. It is essential for maintaining safety and fostering a sense of community.

What is Prevent?

The Prevent strategy is part of the UK's overall counter-terrorism strategy, CONTEST. It aims to stop people from becoming terrorists or supporting terrorism.

Recognizing Risks

Recognizing the signs of radicalization is crucial in preventing terrorism. Here are some signs to watch for:

  • Sudden changes in behavior or ideology
  • Isolation from family and friends
  • Expressing extremist views
  • Accessing extremist content online
